Detailed Description

Adjacent DW_OP_LLVM_fragment should be merged into one.

E.g. #llvm.di_expression<[ DW_OP_LLVM_fragment(32, 32), DW_OP_LLVM_fragment(32, 64) ]> => #llvm.di_expression<[DW_OP_LLVM_fragment(64, 32)]>
